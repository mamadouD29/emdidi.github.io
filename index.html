Mamadou Doumbia

My name is Mamadou Doumbia. I am a Computer Science graduate from Wichita State University in Kansas, United States. I am passionate about software and web development. My love for building and interactive user interfaces led me to work as a front-end developer on Web and Mobile Asset Management applications. Also, I worked on different projects for personal use. Some of those projects are listed below in the work section

Like, any graduate of Computer Science, I started my journey with high-level programming languages, standard markup, stylesheet languages, and graphic editors such as C++, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, SQL, Adobe Photoshop, and Gimp. After my knowledge got stimulated, I learned programming languages, run-time environments, and web development platforms ( application frameworks) like Python, C#, Node.js, and ASP.NET in my spare time.

If you feel interested in my work, contact me for services or follow my social media.
